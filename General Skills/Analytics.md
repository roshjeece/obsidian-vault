Data Analytics:
- Informs decision making across industry
- Leverage analytics to extract insights from data and drive strategic actions
- Five types of analytics:
	1. Descriptive
	2. Diagnostic
	3. Predictive
	4. Prescriptive
	5. Cognitive

Descriptive:
- Data Visualization (charts, graphs, dashboards)
- Summary statistics (mean, median, mode, standard dev)
- Business Intelligence (BI) like Tableau and Power BI

Diagnostic:
- "Why did this happen?"
- Drill-down analytics (breaking down data by category)
- Data mining (finding patterns in large datasets)
- Correlation and regression analysis
