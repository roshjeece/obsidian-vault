- A short simple description of a desired capability
- Written from the perspective of the person who desires the new capability

### User Story (The Why)
- As a "user/persona"
- I want "action"
- So that "outcome"

### Acceptance Criteria (The How)
- Given (how things begin)
- When (action taken)
- Then (outcome of taking action)

### Value of User Stories
- Understanding the who, what, and why of a feature
- Lightweight documentation
- Placeholder for a team conversation
- Definition of done
- Short feedback loops

### Responsibilities of the Balanced Team

###### Product Designers
- Define the User Value
- Ensure every story provides value to the User
- Reflect design intention in Acceptance Criteria and generate the mockup

###### Product Managers
- Sync with team to generate and discuss stories
- Steward and facilitate the story life-cycle
- Continuously prioritizes stories in the backlog
